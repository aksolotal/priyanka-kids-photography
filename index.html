About Priyanka Das

Your Story, My Lens, Endless Memories.

Hello! I'm Priyanka Das, the founder and lead photographer behind **Priyanks Kids Photography**. I'm absolutely passionate about capturing the most precious chapters of your family's journey. From the gentle curves of maternity to the tiny toes of newborns and the infectious giggles of toddlers, I believe every moment is a miracle worth preserving.

My approach is rooted in **patience, warmth, and an unwavering commitment to safety**. Especially with my littlest clients, I create a calm, nurturing environment where both parents and babies feel completely at ease. I understand the nuances of working with infants and active toddlers, ensuring every session is relaxed and enjoyable.

It's an honor to transform these fleeting stages into timeless works of art that you'll cherish for generations. Let's create something beautiful together.
