About Me

Hi, I'm Priyanka

My journey into photography began with the birth of my own child. I discovered a deep passion for capturing the fleeting, beautiful moments of childhood. My style is authentic and minimalistic, focusing on the pure and genuine emotions of your little ones.

I believe in creating a comfortable and fun environment where children can be themselves, allowing their unique personalities to shine through in every photograph.
